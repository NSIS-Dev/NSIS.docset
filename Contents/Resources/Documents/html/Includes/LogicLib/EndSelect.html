EndSelect

Ends an open block of statements started by ${Select}.

Syntax

${EndSelect}

Example

StrCpy $0 1

${Select} $0
    ${Case} "1"
        MessageBox MB_OK "$$0 is 1"
    ${Case} "2"
        MessageBox MB_OK "$$0 isn't 2"
    ${CaseElse}
        MessageBox MB_OK "$$0 isn't anything else"
${EndSelect}
