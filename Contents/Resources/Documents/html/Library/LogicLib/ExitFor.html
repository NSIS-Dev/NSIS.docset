ExitFor

Repeats a block of statements varying the value of a variable.

Syntax:

${ExitFor} expression

Example:

StrCpy $0 ""

${For} $1 1 10
    StrCpy $0 $0$1
    ${If} $1 == 5
        ; let's interrupt this at 5
        ${ExitFor}
    ${EndIf}
${Next}

; $0 = 12345
