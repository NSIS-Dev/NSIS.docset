IfRebootFlag

Checks the reboot flag, and jumps to jumpifset if the reboot flag is set, otherwise jumps to jumpifnot_set. The reboot flag can be set by Delete and Rename, or manually with SetRebootFlag.

Parameters:

jump_if_set [jump_if_not_set]

Example:

IfRebootFlag 0 noreboot
MessageBox MB_YESNO "A reboot is required to finish the installation. Do you wish to reboot now?" IDNO noreboot
Reboot
noreboot:
