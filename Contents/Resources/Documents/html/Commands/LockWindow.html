LockWindow

LockWindow on prevents the main window from redrawing itself upon changes. WhenLockWindow off is used, all controls that weren't redrawn since `LockWindow on will be redrawn. This makes the pages flickering look nicer because now it flickers a group of controls at the same time, instead of one control at a time. The individual control flickering is more noticeable on old computers.

Parameters:

on|off

History:

Added in NSIS v2.0
