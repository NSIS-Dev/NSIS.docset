ShowWindow

Sets the visibility of a window. Possible showstates are the same as Windows ShowWindow function. SW* constants are defined in Include\WinMessages.nsh.

Parameters:

hwnd show_state

Example:

!include WinMessages.nsh
GetDlgItem $0 $HWNDPARENT 1
ShowWindow $0 ${SW_HIDE}
Sleep 1000
ShowWindow $0 ${SW_SHOW}

History:
